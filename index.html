Features We Provided

Alumni Directory

Provide a searchable alumni directory that helps alumni reconnect with their batchmates or find individuals with similar career paths or interests.

Alumni Connections

Provide a networking hub where alumni can connect with each other based on their professional field, interests, or geographical location.

Alumni Donations

Enable alumni to donate funds to the university for various causes like scholarships, research funding, infrastructure development, or special projects.

Mentorship & Guidance

Create a platform where experienced alumni can mentor recent graduates or students by offering career advice, professional development guidance, and industry insights.

Job Portal

Allow alumni to post job openings at their organizations and help other alumni or students find job opportunities. The platform can also facilitate internships or freelance work.
